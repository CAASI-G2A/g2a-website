Who We Are

Grief to Action was an initiative started by the Center for Analytical Approaches to Social Innovation (CAASI) at the University of Pittsburgh. The recent events have ignited conversation and action throughout the whole country to support marginalized and underrepresented minorities in America. We have turned this grief into action by using our skills, tools and resources to develop projects that can help support and empower these groups. The Project Elevate team is a subgroup of Grief to Action. Scroll down to see our team!!

Mission Statement

Project Elevate is a program that strives to promote black businesses through the medium of online reviews. We are launching with the initiative of finding groups on the University of Pittsburgh campus to pledge to purchase goods and services from Black businesses, then provide reviews of their experience on social media and review websites (such as Yelp and Google Reviews). Each group would receive their own page of information and access to data about the Black businesses they are visiting and provide recommendations with what types of businesses and areas they are missing out on! We want to create a closer and supportive relationship between these Black businesses, the university communities and area residents in general.
