Who We Are

Grief to Action was an initiative started by the Center for Analytical Approaches to Social Innovation (CAASI) at the University of Pittsburgh. The recent events have ignited conversation and action throughout the whole country to support marginalized and underrepresented minorities in America. We have turned this grief into action by using our skills, tools and resources to develop projects that can help support and empower these groups.
